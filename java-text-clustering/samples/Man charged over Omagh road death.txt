An 18-year-old man has been charged with dangerous driving, causing death and other related motoring offences. 

He will appear at Enniskillen Magistrates Court on Monday morning. The charges relate to a collision on Doogary Road in Omagh.

Simon Glenn, 26, from the Strabane area, was killed in the accident which happened at about 0410 BST on Saturday.

Two women, aged 48 and 23, and a three year-old girl, were taken to hospital with non-life threatening injuries.
