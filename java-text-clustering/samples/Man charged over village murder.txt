A 22-year-old man has been arrested and charged in connection with the murder of a man in a West Lothian village. 

The body of 27-year-old Jim Tierney was found in a garden in Pumpherston after police were called a disturbance at a house in the village in the early hours of Saturday.

Mr Tierney, of Harrysmuir Terrace in Pumpherston, had suffered multiple stab wounds. 

The 22-year-old man is expected to appear in court on Monday.
